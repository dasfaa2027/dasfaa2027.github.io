Welcome to DASFAA 2027

The International Conference on Database Systems for Advanced Applications (DASFAA) is a leading international forum for research and development in database systems and their advanced applications. DASFAA 2027 will bring together researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to present original results, exchange ideas, and discuss emerging directions in data management.

Topics span the foundations, design, implementation, evaluation, and application of database systems, including data management, data engineering, analytics, knowledge discovery, artificial intelligence for data systems, and large-scale applications.

Topics of Interest

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

Foundations & Theories

Foundations, algorithms, models, and theory for database systems and data management.

ML/DL & Statistical Methods

Machine learning, deep learning, and statistical methods for big data.

Heterogeneous Data Management

Management and analysis of text, semi-structured, spatio-temporal, streaming, graph, web, and multimedia data.

Systems & Platforms

Database systems, platforms, and infrastructures for big data, including parallel, distributed, federated, secure, and privacy-aware data management.

Modeling, Visualization & Recommendations

Data management and analytics for modeling, visualization, personalization, and recommendation.

CPS & Complex Time-Evolving Networks

Data management for cyber-physical systems and complex, time-evolving networks.

LLM-Driven

Database systems and data management with large language models and foundation models.

Novel Applications

Novel database and data management applications in social sciences, physical sciences, engineering, life sciences, climate science, web, finance, healthcare, and other domains.

We particularly encourage submissions on emerging topics such as AI-native data systems, data governance, responsible data analytics, cloud-native databases, multi-modal data management, and heterogeneous data integration.
